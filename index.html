ABOUT
We introduce BIM SIGN, a multimodal and multiview continuous sign language dataset, consisting of a parallel corpus of sign language videos and a set of corresponding modalities including speech, transcripts, and depth.

A subset was further recorded in a studio enabling detailed 3D pose estimation.

This dataset is publicly available for research purposes only.
Example: RGB, depth, pose, and gloss annotation
