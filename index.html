About the Project

What is BIM-SIGN?

BIM stands for Bahasa Isyarat Malaysia — the official Malaysian Sign Language used by the Deaf community in Malaysia. Despite being a rich and expressive language, BIM has long lacked the computational resources needed for modern AI research.

BIM-SIGN is the first publicly available word-level video dataset for Malaysian Sign Language, designed to support research in sign language recognition (SLR) and sign language translation (SLT).
